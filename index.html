Our Story

More Than a
Coffee Shop

Welcome to Kixi Café, a coffee lover's dream nestled in the heart of Mizner Park, Boca Raton. We source our beans exclusively from Wells Coffee — a local roaster we love — and pair every cup with our legendary Japanese soufflé pancakes.

Founded in 2020, Kixi has grown into a beloved community hub. Whether you're catching up with friends on the dog-friendly patio or discovering a new brunch obsession, every visit is a little bit magical.
